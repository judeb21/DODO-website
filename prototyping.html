PROTOTYPING FOR BUSINESSES

Learn to build Customer-friendly products & services for profitability

A 4 DAY PRACTICAL WORKSHOP FOR BUSINESS OWNERS, STRATEGY TEAMS AND ENTREPRENEURS, BUILT TO EQUIP YOU WITH THE KNOWLEDGE AND TOOLS YOU NEED TO BRING BETTER PRODUCTS TO MARKET.

What Is Prototyping?

Prototyping is the process of testing your products and services quickly and cost-effectively with customers to avoid product failures in market and ensure profitability and satisfied customers.

    Workshop Breakdown

    The prototyping workshop runs for 4 days; 2 weekends with in-field practical (2 Fridays and 2 Saturdays), allowing a week during the process, to test with real users.

    DAY 1 (10.00AM - 5.00PM)

    Understanding the Basics. We’ll take you through the entire prototyping process with detailed real life case studies. Dive into the workshop proper as you work through the prototyping framework using your own real life products and services.

    DAY 2 (10.00AM - 5.00PM)

    Prototyping proper I. Learn the principles that make testing with customers successful, and be able to immediately put your knowledge to test with practicals. Understand how to build products and services as quickly as you can to get useful customer feedback.

    FIELD TESTING DURING THE WEEK*

    DAY 3 | ESTABLISH (10.00AM - 5.00PM)

    Armed with actions steps from the earlier phase, we begin to execute following a well mapped out framework. Along with the solution, we will together build a pitch for buy-in from sponsors, executives and stakeholders.

    DAY 4 (10.00AM - 5.00PM)

    Iterate and improve. Build off of the feedback you’ve gotten from real customers using techniques and frameworks geared to enhance your final solution for the market acceptance.

    *You’ll spend the week in-between the classes in the real world, testing your product or service with real customers based on the tools we’ve given you. You’ll return with results that we can then build off of in the following week’s workshop.
